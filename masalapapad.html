Masala Papad

A quick and crispy Indian snack topped with spicy masala mix.

Ingredients:
  • 2 Papads (roasted or fried)
  • 1 Small Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Small Tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 Small Cucumber (finely chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (finely chopped, optional)
  • Fresh Coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Chaat Masala
  • 1/4 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice (few drops)
Process:
  1. Roast or fry the papads until crispy.
  2. In a bowl, mix onion, tomato, cucumber, green chili, and coriander leaves.
  3. Add salt, chili powder, and chaat masala; mix well.
  4. Place the papad on a plate and spread the mixture evenly over it.
  5. Drizzle lemon juice on top and serve immediately.
