Curd Salad (Dahi Salad)

A refreshing, healthy, and easy-to-make salad with yogurt and vegetables.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Fresh Curd (Yogurt)
  • 1 Cucumber (chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Carrot (grated)
  • 1 Green Chili (finely chopped, optional)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tsp Roasted Cumin Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • A pinch of Black Pepper
Process:
  1. Whisk the curd in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add cucumber, tomato, onion, carrot, and green chili.
  3. Mix well and season with salt, pepper, and roasted cumin powder.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  5. Serve chilled as a side dish or a healthy snack.
