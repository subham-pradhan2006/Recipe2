Dum Biryani Recipe

A fragrant and flavorful layered rice dish cooked on dum.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken / Mutton
  • 2 Large Onions (sliced & fried)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 Cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole spices (Cinnamon, Cardamom, Bay Leaf, Cloves)
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • Fresh Coriander & Mint leaves
  • 4 tbsp Ghee / Oil
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Salt to taste
Process:
  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes, then parboil with whole spices.
  2. Marinate chicken/mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and biryani masala for 1 hour.
  3. Cook the marinated meat with onions and tomatoes until tender.
  4. Layer rice and meat in a pot, sprinkle fried onions, mint, coriander, ghee, and saffron milk.
  5. Seal with a lid and cook on low flame (dum) for 20â€“25 minutes.
  6. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
