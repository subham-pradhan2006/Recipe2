Pasta Recipe

A simple and tasty homemade pasta dish.

Ingredients:
  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 Cup Tomato Sauce
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Fresh Basil for garnish
Process:
  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© onion & garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in the boiled pasta and season with salt & pepper.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with fresh basil.
