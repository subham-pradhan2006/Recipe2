Maggi Noodles

A quick and tasty 2-minute snack loved by everyone.

Ingredients:
  • 1 Packet Maggi Noodles
  • 1½ Cup Water
  • Maggi Masala Tastemaker (from packet)
  • 1 Small Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Small Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (optional)
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish
Process:
  1. Heat oil/butter in a pan and sauté onion, tomato, and green chili.
  2. Add 1½ cups water and bring to a boil.
  3. Add Maggi noodles and the Tastemaker masala.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the noodles soften and absorb the flavors.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
